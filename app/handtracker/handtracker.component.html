Welcome to Our Touchless Quiz!

Use hand motions to select your answer!

Each question will have 4 answer to choose from. Here's how it works:

  • To select option #1 (upper-left), place one open hand in front of the camera.
  • To select option #2 (upper-right), place one closed hand in front of the camera.
  • To select option #3 (lower-left), place two open hands in front of the camera.
  • To select option #4 (lower-right), place two closed hands in front of the camera.
  • To move onto the next question, pinch one hand.
  • To submit results, close one hand and use the other to point (upward works best)
  • To restart quiz, open one hand and use the other to pinch
